Single Player Mode

When the player moves he gathers points but must not toutch the balls that will incremently spawn randomly around him. Highscores will be recorded but will not persist after exiting the game.

Two Player Mode

Two Players will go head to head in canvas. The players must try to survive as the balls incremently spawn around them. The first one to die 3 time will lose and the surviving player will win


Player 1 Use W,A,S,D

Player 2 Use The Arrow Keys
